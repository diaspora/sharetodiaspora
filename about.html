Advanced Sharer for diaspora*

Share all teh internetz!

Welcome! You found the website of our little cross-pod sharing application. This was mainly created to embed the share application into Firefox, but it is also possible to use it as a dedicated "share to diaspora*" button in your favorite website.

Firefox Social Provider

Add a native Share button to the Share Services of Firefox. Click "Share This Page" from the browser toolbar to open the save dialog. Just click the following button and choose Enable Services.

Add to Firefox

Bookmarklet

Instead of using several bookmarklets for several pods, you can just drag this one: Share on diaspora* to your bookmarks bar.

Add-on for Firefox

If you do not want to use Firefox' Social API or the bookmarklet, diaspora* Advanced Sharer by Jason Robinson adds a sharing button to Mozilla Firefox.
